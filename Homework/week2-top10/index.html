My "Whys"

Minecraft - This is the ultimate 'Skeletal' builder. Long before I knew what HTML was, I was obsessed with building here.

Portal 2 - The puzzles are the ultimate test of Logic.

Skate 3 - Since I love skateboarding in real life, this is my go-to for relaxing. I love testing the physics Engine—sometimes I even try to 'break' the game just to see how the physics logic reacts.

The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom - The building mechanics are awesome!

Cities: Skylines - Managing a city's layout is really fun.

Marvel's Spider-Man - I'm a huge fan of the Spider-Verse movies, and this game lets me explore that same 'visual storytelling.'

Stardew Valley - It's fun to track time, seeds, and money.

Horizon Zero Dawn - The Sci-Fi setting is incredible. The design in the 'Focus' mode is exactly the kind of professional interface I want to create after I learn about design

Celeste - This game is tough, but it's taught me to keep trying until you reach the summit.

Roblox - I have respect for the classics.
